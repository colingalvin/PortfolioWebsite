About Me

My name is Colin Galvin, and I am a professionally-trained musician turned software developer. After 6.5 years of school and 9 years of performance/teaching experience, I decided that it was time for a new challenge. So, I found a coding bootcamp, applied the same work ethic that I honed after all of the years of practice on my instrument, and came out the other side ready to make my mark on the developer world! Thank you for visiting, and please enjoy everything that my website has to offer!
