About Me

My name is Colin Galvin, and I am a musician turned software developer. After 7 years of school and 9 years of professional performance and teaching experience, I decided that it was time for a new challenge. So, I took the work ethic and critical-thinking skills I had honed after years of teaching in nationally-recognized music programs and decades of practice on my instrument, applied them to learning software design and development, and came out the other side ready to make my mark on the developer world! Thank you for visiting, and please enjoy everything that my website has to offer!
